<p><p>Pipe bursting systems are innovative technologies used for trenchless rehabilitation of underground pipes. Hydraulic Pipe Bursting System uses hydraulic power to fracture and replace old pipes with new ones, while Cable-Pull Pipe Bursting System uses a cable and pulling machine to achieve the same result. Both systems offer advantages such as reduced excavation, minimal disruption, and cost savings compared to traditional pipe replacement methods. These systems are widely used in municipal, industrial, and residential sectors for repairing and upgrading underground utility pipelines.</p></p>
