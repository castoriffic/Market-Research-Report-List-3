<p><p>Flame retardants for fibers are chemicals used to reduce the flammability of materials such as textiles. There are three main types of flame retardants in the market: phosphorous flame retardants, halogen flame retardants, and other flame retardants. Phosphorous flame retardants work by disrupting the combustion process, while halogen flame retardants release gases that inhibit the spread of flames. Other flame retardants may include nitrogen-based compounds or mineral-based additives. Each type of flame retardant has its own unique properties and suitability for different applications.</p></p>
