<p><p>The market for automobile fluid pipe products can be categorized into two types: flexible and rigid. Flexible pipes are made of materials like rubber or plastics and can bend or stretch, allowing for easy installation and maneuverability. They are commonly used for conveying fluids in areas with restricted space. On the other hand, rigid pipes are made of materials like metal or PVC and cannot flex. They are used in applications where durability and stability are crucial, such as high-pressure fluid systems.</p></p>
