TFT Type offers enhanced image quality and response time, making it ideal for high-definition displays. TN Type is cost-effective and commonly used in basic displays. STN Type provides improved contrast and wider viewing angles. HTN Type combines the benefits of TN and STN types, offering good contrast and viewing angles at a reasonable cost.</p></p>
