<p><p>Thickeners market can be classified into various types including inorganic thickener, cellulose ether, synthetic polymer, natural polymer, and its derivatives. Inorganic thickeners are minerals that provide viscosity and stability to various products. Cellulose ether is derived from natural cellulose and used as a thickening agent. Synthetic polymers are man-made chemicals that function as thickeners. Natural polymers are derived from plants or animals and serve as environmentally friendly thickening agents. Derivatives of natural polymers are modified versions to enhance their thickening properties.</p></p>
