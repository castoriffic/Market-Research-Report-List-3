<p><p>The 5G millimeter wave antenna market consists of two main types: Antenna on Chip (AoC) and Antenna in Package (AiP). AoC involves integrating the antenna directly onto a semiconductor chip, offering compact size and lower cost. AiP, on the other hand, involves packaging the antenna separately, providing flexibility in design and performance optimization. Both types cater to the growing demand for high-speed data transmission in 5G networks, with each offering unique advantages to suit different market requirements.</p></p>
