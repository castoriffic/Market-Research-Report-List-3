<p><p>Feed mycotoxin binders are additives used in animal feed to prevent the harmful effects of mycotoxins. Different types of binders include HSCAS, Bentonite, Zeolites, and Polysaccharides. HSCAS binders are natural clays with a high affinity for mycotoxins. Bentonite binders are clay minerals with a large surface area for binding mycotoxins. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates that trap mycotoxins. Polysaccharides are carbohydrates that bind mycotoxins in the gut. Each type of binder offers unique benefits in mitigating mycotoxin contamination in animal feed.</p></p>
