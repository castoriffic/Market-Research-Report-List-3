<p><strong>The Cubic Boron Nitride Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CBN Monocrystalline</li><li>CBN Micro Mist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) comes in two main types - CBN Monocrystalline and CBN Micro Mist. CBN Monocrystalline is a single crystal form that offers high thermal conductivity, hardness, and abrasion resistance, making it ideal for high-performance cutting tools. On the other hand, CBN Micro Mist consists of tiny CBN particles dispersed in a coolant fluid, allowing for easy application in grinding and polishing operations. Both types of CBN offer superior performance and are widely used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
