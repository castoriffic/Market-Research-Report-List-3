<p><ul><li>Rail Lighting</li><li>Post Lighting</li><li>Stair Lighting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deck light market includes three main types: rail lighting, post lighting, and stair lighting. Rail lighting is designed to attach to the deck railing, providing ambient lighting around the perimeter of the deck. Post lighting is installed on top of deck posts, offering a decorative element as well as functional illumination. Stair lighting is made to be installed on deck stairs, enhancing safety by lighting up each step. These different types cater to various deck layouts and design preferences, catering to a wide range of customers in the market.</p></p>
