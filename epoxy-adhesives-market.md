<p><p>Epoxy adhesives are available in various types based on their formulations and usage. One-component epoxies are pre-catalyzed and ready to use, offering convenience and ease of application. Two-component epoxies require mixing of resin and hardener before application, providing a strong bond and versatility in terms of curing time. Other types of epoxy adhesives include structural and semi-structural epoxies for specialized applications. Each type has its unique advantages and is suitable for different bonding requirements in various industries.</p></p>
