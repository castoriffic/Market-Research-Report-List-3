<p><p>Lime oil is available in three main market types: distilled, expressed, and essence. Distilled lime oil is obtained through the process of steam distillation, which involves separating the oil from the plant material using heat and water vapor. Expressed lime oil is extracted through a mechanical process where the oil is squeezed out of the fruit peel. Essence market refers to the synthetic version of lime oil made in a lab. These different market types cater to varying preferences and applications within the industry.</p></p>
