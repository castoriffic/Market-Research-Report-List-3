<p><p>Thermoset filler market types including oxides, silicates, and hydroxides are materials commonly used to improve the properties of thermoset resins. Oxides such as alumina and silica provide high thermal stability and electrical insulation, making them suitable for electronic applications. Silicates, like calcium silicate, offer improved flame resistance and mechanical strength. Hydroxides such as magnesium hydroxide act as flame retardants by releasing water vapor when heated. Each type of filler plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance of thermoset materials in various industries.</p></p>
