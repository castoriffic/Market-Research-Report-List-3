<p><ul><li>Emulsion Polymerization</li><li>Suspension Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDF lithium ion batteries binders come in two main types of market processes: emulsion polymerization and suspension polymerization. In emulsion polymerization, PVDF binders are produced by dispersing monomers in an aqueous phase with the help of emulsifiers, resulting in the formation of polymer particles. Suspension polymerization, on the other hand, involves the dispersion of monomers in a liquid phase with the aid of stabilizers, forming polymer particles that are then separated from the reaction mixture. Both processes play a key role in the production of PVDF binders for lithium ion batteries.</p></p>
