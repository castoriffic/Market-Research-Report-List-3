<p><p>Food packaging wax market consists of two main types: natural wax and mineral wax. Natural waxes, such as beeswax and carnauba wax, are derived from plants or animals and are biodegradable and environmentally friendly. On the other hand, mineral waxes, like paraffin wax, are derived from petroleum or mineral sources and have a higher melting point. Both types of wax are commonly used in food packaging to provide a protective coating, enhance appearance, and prolong shelf life.</p></p>
