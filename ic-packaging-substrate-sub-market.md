<p><strong>The IC Packaging Substrate (SUB) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Substrate</li><li>Inorganic Substrate</li><li>Composite Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC packaging substrate market consists of three main types: Organic, Inorganic, and Composite substrates. Organic substrates are made from materials like epoxy resins and are cost-effective but have limited thermal conductivity. Inorganic substrates, like ceramics, offer higher thermal conductivity but are more expensive. Composite substrates combine the benefits of both organic and inorganic materials, offering a balance between cost and performance. Each type of substrate caters to different needs in terms of cost, performance, and thermal management in the IC packaging industry.</p></p>
