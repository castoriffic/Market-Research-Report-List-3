<p><strong>The AMOLED Electroluminescent Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emission Layer Materials</li><li>Common Layer Materials</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AMOLED electroluminescent materials market is segmented into two main types: Emission Layer Materials and Common Layer Materials. Emission Layer Materials are responsible for generating light when an electric current passes through them, creating color in OLED displays. Common Layer Materials function as a charge transport layer for the emission layer, aiding in the efficient flow of electrons and holes to create the desired color and brightness in the display. These materials are essential components in the production of high-quality OLED screens.</p></p>
