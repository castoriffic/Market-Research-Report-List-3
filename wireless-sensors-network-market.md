<p><p>Wireless Sensor Network (WSN) technology is divided into three main market types: MEMS, CMOS-based sensors, and LED sensors. MEMS or Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems sensors are tiny mechanical devices often used in consumer electronics. CMOS-based sensors are similar to MEMS but are fabricated using CMOS technology, making them more energy-efficient and cost-effective for large-scale deployment. LED sensors combine light-emitting diodes with sensors to enable various applications such as environmental monitoring and smart lighting. These market types represent different technologies that contribute to the growth and innovation of wireless sensor networks across various industries.</p></p>
