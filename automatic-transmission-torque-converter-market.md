<p><ul><li>Single-stage Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic transmission torque converter market consists of two main types: single-stage torque converter and multistage torque converter. A single-stage torque converter has a single turbine and stator, while a multistage torque converter has multiple sets of turbines and stators. Single-stage torque converters are simpler in design and offer lower efficiency, while multistage torque converters are more complex and provide higher efficiency. Both types are used in automatic transmissions to transmit torque from the engine to the transmission for smooth and efficient shifting of gears.</p></p>
