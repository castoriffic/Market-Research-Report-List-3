<p><p>Neutral cleaner market types can be classified into two categories: concentrate and low concentration. Concentrate neutral cleaners are highly potent and require dilution before use. They are commonly used in commercial settings, such as hospitals or hotels, where large quantities of cleaner are needed. Low concentration neutral cleaners, on the other hand, are ready-to-use products that do not require further dilution. They are more suitable for residential or small-scale cleaning needs, providing convenience and ease of use for everyday cleaning tasks.</p></p>
