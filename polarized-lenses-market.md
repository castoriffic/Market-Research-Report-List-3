<p><p>Polarized lenses are available in two main types: resin and glass. Resin lenses are lighter, more durable, and less likely to shatter compared to glass lenses. They are also more affordable and provide superior UV protection. Glass lenses, on the other hand, offer better optical clarity and scratch resistance. They are often preferred for high-end sunglasses due to their premium feel and ability to resist smudges. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and needs in the polarized lenses market.</p></p>
