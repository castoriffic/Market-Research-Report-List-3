<p><ul><li>Single Zone</li><li>Dual Zone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermostatic wine coolers are available in two main types - single zone and dual zone. Single zone coolers have one temperature setting throughout the entire unit, making them ideal for storing one type of wine at a time. On the other hand, dual zone coolers have two separate compartments with independent temperature controls, allowing for the storage of different types of wine at their optimal temperatures simultaneously. Both types offer different benefits and cater to different preferences and needs of wine enthusiasts.</p></p>
