<p><p>Polyurethane films made from polyester offer two main types in the market - Pure Polyester PU Film and Polyether-Polyester PU Film. Pure Polyester PU Film is renowned for its high tensile strength, durability, and excellent resistance to abrasion and chemicals. On the other hand, Polyether-Polyester PU Film combines the flexibility and elasticity of polyether with the strength and abrasion resistance of polyester. Both types of films are widely used in various industries such as automotive, packaging, and textiles for their protective and decorative properties.</p></p>
