<p><p>Trailer wire connectors are available in various types to suit different trailer wiring setups. 4-way connectors are the most basic, providing connections for lights, brakes, and ground. 5-way connectors add an additional wire for auxiliary power. 6-way connectors include connections for reverse lights. 7-way connectors are the most versatile, accommodating all types of trailer wiring needs, including brakes, backup lights, and auxiliary power. Each type caters to specific trailer requirements, allowing for safe and efficient towing.</p></p>
