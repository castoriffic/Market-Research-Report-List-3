<p><ul><li>Rule Based</li><li>Knowledge Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robotic automation process is classified into two main types: rule-based and knowledge-based markets. Rule-based automation involves implementing software robots that follow a set of predefined rules and instructions to perform repetitive, rule-based tasks. These tasks are usually well-structured and do not require human intervention. On the other hand, knowledge-based automation utilizes artificial intelligence and machine learning to enable software robots to analyze and understand unstructured data, make informed decisions, and perform complex tasks that require human-like intelligence.</p></p>
