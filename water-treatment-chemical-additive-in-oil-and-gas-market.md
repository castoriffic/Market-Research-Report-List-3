Coagulants and flocculants help remove impurities and solids from water, while biocides and disinfectants eliminate harmful microorganisms. Chelating agents bind with metal ions to prevent scale formation, anti-foaming agents reduce foam in water treatment processes, and pH adjusters and stabilizers maintain the balance of water chemistry. Other additives may serve specific purposes based on the unique needs of each operation.</p></p>
