<p><strong>The Laser Markable Label Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antistatic</li><li>Non-antistatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser markable label material can be categorized into two types based on their antistatic properties: antistatic and non-antistatic. Antistatic label material helps to prevent the buildup of static electricity, reducing the risk of damage to sensitive electronic components. Non-antistatic label material, on the other hand, does not have this feature and may be more suitable for applications where static electricity is not a concern. Both types of label material can be used for laser marking applications to create high-quality, permanent markings on various surfaces.</p></p>
