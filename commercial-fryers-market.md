<p><p>Commercial fryers come in two main types - countertop and floor-standing. They are often used in smaller establishments such as food trucks, cafes, and small restaurants. Floor-standing fryers are larger and are typically used in larger kitchens such as in hotels, large restaurants, and catering facilities. They offer higher capacities and are ideal for high-volume frying operations.</p></p>
