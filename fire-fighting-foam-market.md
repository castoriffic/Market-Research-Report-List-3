<p><p>Fire fighting foam is used to suppress fires by cooling and coating the fuel surface, preventing oxygen from reaching it. There are three main types of fire fighting foam: protein foam, synthetic foam, and Class A foam. Protein foam is made from natural proteins and is effective against flammable liquids. Synthetic foam is made from synthetic materials and is effective against polar solvents. Class A foam is designed for use on Class A fires, which involve ordinary combustible materials such as wood and paper.</p></p>
