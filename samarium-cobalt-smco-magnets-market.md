<p><p>Samarium-Cobalt (SmCo) magnets are classified into two main types: SmCo5 and Sm2Co17. SmCo5 magnets are made of samarium and cobalt in a 1:5 ratio, while Sm2Co17 magnets have a 2:17 ratio. SmCo5 magnets have lower energy products and coercivities compared to Sm2Co17 magnets, making them suitable for moderate temperature applications. On the other hand, Sm2Co17 magnets have higher energy products and coercivities, making them ideal for high-performance applications in harsh environments. Both types of magnets are highly resistant to corrosion and have excellent thermal stability.</p></p>
