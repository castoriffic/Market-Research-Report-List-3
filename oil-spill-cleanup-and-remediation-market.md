<p><p>Oil spill cleanup and remediation market involves several types of techniques including physical, chemical & biological, and mechanical methods. Physical methods involve the use of barriers, booms, and skimmers to contain and remove the oil from the water surface. Chemical & biological methods involve the use of dispersants and bacteria to break down the oil. Mechanical methods include vacuum trucks and sorbent materials to collect and remove oil from the affected areas. Each type of method plays a crucial role in effectively cleaning up and remediating oil spills.</p></p>
