<p><p>Proton therapy instruments utilize different types of particle accelerators to deliver precise radiation treatment to cancer patients. Synchrotron accelerators use magnetic fields to steer protons in a circular path at increasing speeds. Cyclotrons produce protons by accelerating charged particles in a spiral path. Synchrocyclotrons combine features of both synchrotron and cyclotron designs. Linear accelerators use microwave technology to accelerate protons in a straight line. Each type of accelerator has its unique advantages and applications in the rapidly growing proton therapy market.</p></p>
