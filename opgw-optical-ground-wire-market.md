<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The OPGW (Optical Ground Wire) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OPGW is classified into two market types based on their structure: Central Tube Structure OPGW and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW. Central Tube Structure OPGW consists of a central stainless steel tube that houses optical fibers wrapped in a layer of metal wires. Layer Stranding Structure OPGW, on the other hand, has optical fibers scattered in multiple layers along with the OPGW structure. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as installation ease, mechanical strength, and signal transmission efficiency.</p></p>
