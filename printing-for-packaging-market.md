<p><p>Printing for packaging market is divided into three main types: Flexography printing, Rotogravure printing, and Digital printing. Flexography printing is commonly used for large volume runs and high-speed production, offering good printing quality on various substrates. Rotogravure printing is ideal for high-quality, long-run printing jobs, known for its vibrant and consistent color reproduction. Digital printing is suitable for short runs, offering quick turnaround times and customization options. Each type caters to different packaging needs based on volume, quality, and flexibility requirements.</p></p>
