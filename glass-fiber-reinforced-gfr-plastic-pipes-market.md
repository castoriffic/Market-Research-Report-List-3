<p><p>Glass-fiber-reinforced (GFR) plastic pipes are commonly found in three main types: polyester, vinyl ester, and epoxy. Polyester pipes are cost-effective and resist corrosion, making them suitable for applications such as wastewater treatment. Vinyl ester pipes offer high chemical resistance and mechanical strength, making them ideal for use in harsh chemical environments. Epoxy pipes are known for their durability and high temperature resistance, making them suitable for hot water and industrial applications. Each type of GFR plastic pipe has unique characteristics that cater to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
