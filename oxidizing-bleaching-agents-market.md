<p><p>In the oxidizing bleaching agents market, the primary types are oxidizing bleach and reducing bleach. Oxidizing bleach involves the use of oxygen-based compounds to break down color molecules and remove stains from fabrics. Reducing bleach, on the other hand, works by chemically altering the structure of color molecules to make them less visible. Both types of bleaching agents are commonly used in laundry detergents, household cleaners, and hair bleaching products to achieve desired whitening and brightening effects.</p></p>
