<p><p>Head-Mount Temperature Transmitters are devices used to convert temperature signals from thermocouples or Resistance Temperature Detectors (RTDs) into standardized signals for measurement or control systems. Thermocouple transmitters are used with thermocouples which generate a voltage proportional to the temperature, while RTD transmitters work with RTDs which change resistance with temperature. Both types are commonly used in industrial processes for accurate temperature measurement and control, with thermocouples being more rugged and versatile, and RTDs providing higher accuracy and stability.</p></p>
