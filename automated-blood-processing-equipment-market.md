The market includes two primary types: automatic blood separator and automatic blood processing system. An automatic blood separator is designed to separate whole blood into various components like red blood cells, plasma, and platelets. On the other hand, an automatic blood processing system integrates multiple functions, such as blood separation, testing, and storage, to provide a complete solution for blood processing and transfusion procedures. Both systems contribute to improving efficiency and safety in blood management.</p></p>
