<p><p>The edible animal fats market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and semi-solid. Liquid animal fats are typically oils extracted from animals and remain in liquid form at room temperature. Solid animal fats, such as lard or tallow, are solid at room temperature. Semi-solid animal fats are those that are in between liquid and solid forms, such as butter or ghee. Each type of animal fat has unique properties and uses in cooking, baking, and food production.</p></p>
