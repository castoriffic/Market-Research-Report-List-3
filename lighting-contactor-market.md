<p><strong>The Lighting Contactor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrically Held</li><li>Mechanically Held</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lighting contactors are used to control the operation of lighting systems in commercial and industrial buildings. There are two main types: Electrically Held and Mechanically Held. Electrically Held contactors are energized when the control circuit is closed, while Mechanically Held contactors remain closed even if power is lost. Electrically Held contactors are more commonly used in applications where the lighting needs to be turned on and off frequently, while Mechanically Held contactors are preferred for applications where safety is a priority.</p></p>
