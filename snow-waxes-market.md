<p><p>Snow waxes are divided into two main market types: Glide and Grip. Glide waxes are designed to reduce friction on the snow, allowing for smoother and faster movement. They are commonly used in skiing and snowboarding. Grip waxes, on the other hand, provide traction and grip on the snow, particularly for cross-country skiing and snowshoeing.
