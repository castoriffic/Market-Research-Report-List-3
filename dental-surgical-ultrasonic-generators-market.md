<p><p>Dental surgical ultrasonic generators are medical devices used in dental surgeries to provide ultrasonic vibrations for various dental procedures. There are two types of generators commonly used: self-excited power source and external excited power source. </p><p>A self-excited power source generator is a stand-alone device that produces the required electrical power to generate ultrasonic vibrations. It is self-sustaining and doesn't rely on any external power source.</p><p>On the other hand, an external excited power source generator requires an external power supply to operate. It uses the supplied electrical power to generate ultrasonic vibrations and perform dental procedures.</p></p>
