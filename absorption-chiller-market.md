<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Absorption Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single effect absorption</li><li>Double effect absorption</li><li>Triple effect absorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Absorption chillers are categorized based on the number of effects they utilize to generate cooling. Single effect absorption chillers use a single generator to produce cooling, making them the simplest and least efficient type. Double effect absorption chillers employ two generators, improving efficiency and reducing energy consumption. Triple effect absorption chillers are the most efficient, using three generators to maximize cooling output while minimizing energy usage. Each type serves a specific market depending on the cooling needs and energy efficiency priorities.</p></p>
