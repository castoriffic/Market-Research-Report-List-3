<p><strong>The Soil Moisture Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FullStop System</li><li>Tensiometers System</li><li>Granular Matrix Sensors System</li><li>Capacitance System</li><li>Other System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Soil Moisture Monitoring Systems consist of different types such as the FullStop System, Tensiometers System, Granular Matrix Sensors System, Capacitance System, and Other Systems. The FullStop System uses sensors to measure soil moisture at different depths. Tensiometers System relies on the soil water potential. Granular Matrix Sensors System uses probes that measure moisture content in the soil. Capacitance System measures the soil's dielectric constant to determine moisture levels. Other Systems include various methods like neutron probes, time domain reflectometry, and gypsum blocks for monitoring soil moisture.</p></p>
