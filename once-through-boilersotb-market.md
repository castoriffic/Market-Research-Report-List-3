<p><p>Once-through boilers (OTB) are a type of boiler that heats and circulates water once through a system of pipes to produce steam efficiently. The OTB market consists of two main types: Horizontal Type and Vertical Type. Horizontal Type OTBs are designed with a horizontal arrangement of the tubes, making them suitable for applications with limited vertical space. Vertical Type OTBs, on the other hand, have a vertical tube arrangement, making them ideal for installations where footprint is a concern. Both types offer efficient steam generation for a variety of industrial applications.</p></p>
