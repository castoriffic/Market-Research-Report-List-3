<p><p>Colonoscope video endoscopes market includes two main types: rigid video endoscopes and flexible video endoscopes. Rigid video endoscopes are straight and inflexible instruments that allow for direct visualization of the colon. They are often used for diagnostic purposes and are inserted through the anus. On the other hand, flexible video endoscopes are more flexible and can navigate the twists and turns of the colon. They are commonly used for therapeutic procedures like biopsy or removal of polyps. Both types of video endoscopes play a crucial role in colonoscopy procedures for detecting and treating colon abnormalities.</p></p>
