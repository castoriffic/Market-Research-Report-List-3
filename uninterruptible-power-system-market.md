<p><p>An Uninterruptible Power System (UPS) market consists of two main types: DC Power Supply and AC Power Supply. DC Power Supply systems utilize direct current to provide backup power during outages, making them ideal for sensitive electronic devices. On the other hand, AC Power Supply systems use alternating current to ensure continuous power supply to critical equipment. Both types of UPS systems play a vital role in safeguarding against power interruptions and ensuring uninterrupted operation of important devices.</p></p>
