<p><p>Sun Control Window Films for Buildings come in three main market types: </p><p>1. Clear (Non-Reflective) films allow natural light to enter while blocking UV rays to protect interiors from fading and reduce glare.</p><p>2. Dyed (Non-Reflective) films absorb solar energy and reduce heat transfer, making buildings more energy efficient.</p><p>3. Vacuum Coated (Reflective) films provide both heat and glare reduction by reflecting sunlight away from the building. These films are more effective in hot climates where reducing solar heat gain is a priority.</p></p>
