<p><strong>The Vibration-absorbing Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compression Type</li><li>Shear Type</li><li>Compound Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vibration-absorbing adhesives can be classified into three main types: Compression Type, Shear Type, and Compound Type. Compression Type adhesives work by absorbing vibrations through compression between two surfaces. Shear Type adhesives dissipate vibrations through sheer forces acting parallel to the surface. Compound Type adhesives combine both compression and shear mechanisms to provide comprehensive vibration absorption. Each type offers unique advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
