<p><strong>The 8th Generation OLED Deposition Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>In-line deposition equipment for 8th generation OLED technology involves a continuous production process where multiple steps such as material deposition, patterning, and encapsulation are carried out in a single automated system. Off-line deposition equipment, on the other hand, involves separate machines for each step in the production process, allowing for more flexibility and customization. In-line equipment is preferred for high-volume manufacturing due to its efficiency and lower production cost, while off-line equipment is ideal for smaller production runs and research purposes.</p></p>
