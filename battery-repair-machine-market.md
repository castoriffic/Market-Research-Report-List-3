<p><p>Battery repair machines come in different types, including pulse repair, step wave repair, and plasma repair. Pulse repair machines use short pulses of high current to rejuvenate batteries by breaking up sulfation. Step wave repair machines deliver waves of varying frequencies and amplitudes to desulfate batteries and improve their performance. Plasma repair machines use ionized gas to remove sulfation and restore battery capacity. Each type of machine offers unique benefits and can help extend the lifespan of batteries.</p></p>
