<p><p>Jet fuel additives are essential components used to enhance the performance and safety of aviation fuel. Icing inhibitors prevent the formation of ice crystals during flight. Antioxidants prevent fuel oxidation and degradation. Antiknock additives improve fuel combustion efficiency. Metal deactivators prevent metal corrosion in the fuel system. Conductivity improvers enhance the fuel's ability to dissipate static electricity. Corrosion inhibitors protect fuel system components from corrosion. These various types of additives play a critical role in ensuring the overall quality and reliability of jet fuel for aircraft operations.</p></p>
