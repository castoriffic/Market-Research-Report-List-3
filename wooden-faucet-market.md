<p><p>Wooden faucet market consists of two main types: Surface Wood faucets and All Wood faucets. Surface Wood faucets have a wooden surface coating on the outside while the inner structure is made of a different material like metal or plastic. On the other hand, All Wood faucets are entirely made of wood, including the inner components. Surface Wood faucets are typically more affordable and durable, while All Wood faucets offer a unique aesthetic and eco-friendly option for consumers looking for a natural feel in their homes.</p></p>
