<p><strong>The Household Mini Rice Cookers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Rice Cookers</li><li>IH (Induction Heating) Rice Cookers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traditional Rice Cookers are the most basic type and typically use a heating plate at the bottom of the pot to cook rice. They are typically more affordable and work well for everyday use. On the other hand, IH (Induction Heating) Rice Cookers use electromagnetic induction to heat the entire pot, resulting in more even and efficient cooking. They are usually more expensive but offer advanced features such as precise temperature control and multiple cooking options. Both types cater to different preferences and budgets in the household mini rice cookers market.</p></p>
