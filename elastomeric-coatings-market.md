<p><strong>The Elastomeric Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Elastic Coatings</li><li>Emulsion Elastic Coatings</li><li>Inorganic Polymer Elastic Coatings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Elastomeric coatings market comprises three main types: Solvent elastic coatings, Emulsion elastic coatings, and Inorganic polymer elastic coatings. Solvent elastic coatings are formulated with solvents to provide flexibility and durability. Emulsion elastic coatings are water-based and environmentally friendly, offering excellent adhesion and flexibility. Inorganic polymer elastic coatings are made with inorganic materials for superior durability and weather resistance. Each type of elastomeric coating offers unique properties to suit different application needs in the construction and automotive industries.</p></p>
