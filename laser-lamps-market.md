<p><strong>The Laser Lamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser lamps market can be divided into two main types: pulsed and continuous. Pulsed laser lamps emit short bursts of high-intensity light, making them ideal for applications that require precise energy delivery in a short period of time. Continuous laser lamps, on the other hand, emit a steady stream of light, which is more suitable for applications that require a constant source of illumination. Both types of laser lamps have their own unique advantages and are used in a variety of industries including manufacturing, healthcare, and research.</p></p>
