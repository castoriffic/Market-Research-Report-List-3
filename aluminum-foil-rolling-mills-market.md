<p><p>Aluminum foil rolling mills are classified into three types based on the stage of the rolling process they are involved in. Intermediate aluminum foil rolling mills further reduce the thickness of the material after roughing. Finishing aluminum foil rolling mills are responsible for achieving the final desired thickness and surface quality of the aluminum foil. Each type of mill plays a crucial role in producing high-quality aluminum foil products.</p></p>
