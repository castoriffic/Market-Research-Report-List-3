Radial Tires are designed with ply cords laid at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, providing better fuel efficiency and longer tread life. Bias Tires have ply cords laid diagonally across the tire, offering a smoother ride but less fuel efficiency. Composite Tires combine different materials for enhanced performance. Other market types may include specialty tires designed for specific purposes such as off-roading or high-speed driving.</p></p>
