<p><p>Airport moving walkways come in two main types: belt type and pallet type. Belt type walkways have a continuous loop of a moving belt that passengers stand on and are common in airports for moving people efficiently. Pallet type walkways have individual platforms or pallets that passengers stand on or walk across, providing a smoother ride compared to belt type walkways. Both types of walkways are used in airports to provide convenience and efficiency for passengers moving between terminals or gates.</p></p>
