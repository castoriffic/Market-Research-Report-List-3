The rising prevalence of chronic diseases, such as cardiovascular diseases, orthopedic disorders, and others, has led to a surge in the demand for biocompatible plastics for medical devices and implants. Additionally, the growing geriatric population worldwide is further driving the market growth, as elderly individuals require more medical treatments and surgeries, thereby increasing the demand for biocompatible plastics.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing focus on sustainable and eco-friendly materials is also boosting the demand for biocompatible plastics. <p><strong>The Biocompatible Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PEEK</li><li>PPSU</li><li>POM</li><li>PP</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biocompatible plastics market comprises various types such as PEEK, PPSU, POM, PP, and others. PEEK (Polyether ether ketone) is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its excellent mechanical strength and chemical resistance. PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone) is a durable plastic with high temperature resistance and good electrical properties. POM (Polyoxymethylene) is a dimensionally stable plastic suitable for precision parts due to its low friction and wear resistance. PP (Polypropylene) is a versatile thermoplastic used in packaging and medical devices due to its good chemical resistance. The "others" category includes additional biocompatible plastics like PLA (Polylactic Acid) and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride).</p></p>
