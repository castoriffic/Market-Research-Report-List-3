<p><p>Nemacide market is divided into various types based on the active ingredients used in the nematicides. Fumigants, such as methyl bromide, work by releasing gases that kill nematodes in the soil. Organophosphates, like fenamiphos, disrupt the nervous system of nematodes. Carbamates, such as aldicarb, also target the nervous system but in a different way. Bio-based nematicides use natural ingredients to control nematode populations. Other types of nematicides may use different mechanisms of action to target and eliminate nematodes.</p></p>
