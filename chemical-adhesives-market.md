<p><p>Chemical adhesives are classified into several types based on their composition and properties. Water-based adhesives feature water as a primary solvent, offering environmental benefits. Solvent-based adhesives utilize organic solvents for bonding purposes. Hot-melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and heated to form a liquid for bonding. Other types of adhesives include pressure-sensitive adhesives, which only require light pressure for bonding. Each type of adhesive serves different industries and applications, offering unique benefits and characteristics.</p></p>
