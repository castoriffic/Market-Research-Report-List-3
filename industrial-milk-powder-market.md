<p><p>Industrial Milk Powder Market includes two main types: Whole Milk Powder and Skim Milk Powder. Whole Milk Powder is made by evaporating milk until it is completely dry, containing all the nutrients and fat that are found in milk. Skim Milk Powder, on the other hand, is made by removing the fat from milk before turning it into powder. Both types of milk powder are widely used in the food and beverage industry for various purposes such as baking, confectionery, and dairy products.</p></p>
