<p><p>SOC Reporting Services refers to an evaluation of a company's internal controls and processes conducted by a third-party auditor. SOC1 reports focus on financial reporting controls, while SOC2 reports cover security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. SOC2+ reports are similar to SOC2 but may have additional criteria. SOC3 reports are summarized versions of SOC2 reports designed for public consumption and provide assurance over the same criteria. All these reports aim to enhance transparency and build trust between service providers and their customers regarding the effectiveness of their control measures.</p></p>
