<p><p>Gooseneck hitches are categorized into two main types based on their installation method: above-bed installation and under-bed installation. Above-bed installation hitches are mounted on top of the truck bed, making them easily accessible but taking up space in the bed when not in use. Under-bed installation hitches are mounted beneath the truck bed, providing a cleaner appearance and leaving the bed free for other cargo. Both types offer secure towing capabilities but differ in terms of installation and bed space usage.</p></p>
