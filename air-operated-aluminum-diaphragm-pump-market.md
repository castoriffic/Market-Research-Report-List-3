<p><p>Air operated aluminum diaphragm pumps are classified into two main types: single acting and double acting pumps. Single-acting pumps utilize air pressure to pump liquid in one direction, while double-acting pumps operate in two directions, allowing for continuous fluid transfer. Single-acting pumps are typically used in applications with lower flow rates and pressures, while double-acting pumps are suitable for high-flow and high-pressure applications. Understanding the difference between these two types is essential for selecting the appropriate pump for specific operational requirements.</p></p>
