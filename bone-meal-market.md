<p><p>Bone Meal Market Types include Boiled Bone Meal, Rough Bone Meal, and Steamed Bone Meal. Boiled Bone Meal is produced by boiling the bones to remove fat and moisture, resulting in a cleaner and more concentrated product. Rough Bone Meal is made by grinding the bones into a coarse powder, while Steamed Bone Meal is produced by steaming the bones at high temperatures to increase nutrient availability. These different types cater to various agricultural and horticultural needs, providing essential minerals for plant growth.</p></p>
