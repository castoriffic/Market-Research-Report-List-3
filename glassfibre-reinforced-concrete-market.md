<p><p>The Glassfibre Reinforced Concrete market consists of three main types of processes: Spray Process, Premix Process, and Hybrid Process. Spray Process involves spraying a mixture of concrete and glass fibers onto a mold, resulting in a lightweight and durable product. Premix Process involves pre-blended materials poured into molds, offering precision and consistency. Hybrid Process combines both spray and premix methods, providing a balance of strength and flexibility. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
