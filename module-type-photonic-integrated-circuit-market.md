<p><strong>The Module Type Photonic Integrated Circuit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Photonic Integrated Circuit</li><li>Active Photonic Integrated Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Module Type Photonic Integrated Circuits (PICs) are categorized into two main types: Passive Photonic Integrated Circuits and Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. Passive PICs do not require an external power source and mainly consist of components like waveguides, couplers, and filters for signal routing and manipulation. On the other hand, Active PICs include active components like lasers, modulators, and detectors that require an external power source to amplify and control signals. Both types play a crucial role in the development of advanced optical communication systems.</p></p>
