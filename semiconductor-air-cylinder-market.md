<p><ul><li>Double Acting</li><li>Single Acting</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor air cylinders are categorized into three main types: double-acting cylinders, single-acting cylinders, and others. Double-acting cylinders use air pressure to extend and retract the piston, providing force in both directions. Single-acting cylinders rely on air pressure to extend the piston, with a spring or other mechanism used to retract it. The "others" category may include specialty cylinders with unique operating principles or features beyond the basic double or single-acting design.</p></p>
