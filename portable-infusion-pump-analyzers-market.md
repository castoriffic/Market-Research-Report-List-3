<p><p>Portable infusion pump analyzers are devices that are used to test, calibrate, and validate the performance of portable infusion pumps. This, in turn, is driving the need for analyzers to ensure the accurate and safe functioning of these pumps.</p><p>Another factor contributing to market growth is the stringent regulations and standards imposed by regulatory bodies, such as the FDA and European Medicines Agency, for the quality control of portable infusion pumps.
