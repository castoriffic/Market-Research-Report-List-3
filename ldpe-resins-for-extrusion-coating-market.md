<p><p>LDPE Resins for extrusion coating market is categorized into two main types based on the manufacturing process: Tubular process and Autoclave process. In the tubular process, LDPE resins are melted in a long tube and extruded into a coating, making it ideal for high-speed applications. On the other hand, the autoclave process involves using a pressurized vessel to melt and extrude LDPE resins, resulting in a higher product purity and consistency. Both processes cater to different market needs and applications within the extrusion coating industry.</p></p>
