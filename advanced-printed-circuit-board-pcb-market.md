<p><p>The Advanced Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market consists of three main types: Single-Sided PCB, Double-Sided PCB, and Multi-Layer PCB. Single-Sided PCBs have components and connections on one side, Double-Sided PCBs have them on both sides, while Multi-Layer PCBs have multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched together with insulating layers in between. Each type offers varying levels of complexity and functionality, catering to different requirements for electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
