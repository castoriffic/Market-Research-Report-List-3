<p><p>Hardwood flooring market types include real-wood floor, engineered floor, and laminated floor. Real-wood floor is made entirely of solid wood, offering a timeless and natural look. Engineered floor is made up of multiple layers of wood with a top layer of hardwood, providing greater stability and moisture resistance. Laminated floor features a printed image of wood grain on a fiberboard core, covered with a transparent protective layer. Each type has its own benefits and appeal to different preferences and needs within the flooring market.</p></p>
