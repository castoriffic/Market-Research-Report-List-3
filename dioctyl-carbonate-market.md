<p><p>Dioctyl Carbonate (DOC) is a colorless, odorless, and flammable liquid compound with the chemical formula C18H34O3. It is commonly used as a versatile solvent for various resins, polymers, and chemicals. It is also used as a plasticizer for polymers, specifically polyvinyl chloride (PVC), to enhance flexibility and reduce brittleness. Additionally, DOC finds applications in the manufacturing of coatings, adhesives, lubricants, and cosmetics.</p><p>The Dioctyl Carbonate Market is projected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
