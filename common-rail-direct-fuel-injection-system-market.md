<p><p>The Common Rail Direct Fuel Injection System comes in two types: Solenoid Type and Piezo Type. Solenoid type injectors operate by using a magnetic coil to open and close the injector valve, controlling the fuel flow. On the other hand, Piezo type injectors operate by utilizing a piezoelectric crystal to create precise and rapid fuel injections. Both types offer precise control over fuel delivery, resulting in improved engine performance, efficiency, and reduced emissions in various applications such as automobiles and industrial machinery.</p></p>
