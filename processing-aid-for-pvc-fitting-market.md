<p><p>Processing Aid for PVC Fitting market types include Prill and Powder forms. Prill processing aid is in spherical form, providing easy handling and dissolving properties. It is commonly used in PVC fitting manufacturing for its quick dispersal and effective compatibility with other additives. Powder processing aid, on the other hand, is in powdered form, offering versatility in application methods and blending with PVC formulations. Both Prill and Powder processing aids cater to the diverse needs of the PVC fitting industry, ensuring smooth processing and improved product quality.</p></p>
