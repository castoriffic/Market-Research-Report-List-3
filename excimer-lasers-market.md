<p><p>Excimer lasers are categorized based on their wavelength output, with three main market types: less than 200 nm, 200 nm – 300 nm, and more than 300 nm. Excimer lasers operating at less than 200 nm are typically utilized in applications requiring extremely high precision, such as semiconductor lithography. Those with a wavelength between 200 nm and 300 nm are commonly used in medical industries for procedures like Lasik eye surgery. Excimer lasers with a wavelength above 300 nm are often employed in industrial applications like material processing and microfabrication.</p></p>
