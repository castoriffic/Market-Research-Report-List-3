<p><ul><li>Dry Powder Mask</li><li>Lyophilized Stock Solution Mask</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Freeze drying masks are available in two main types: dry powder mask and lyophilized stock solution mask. Dry powder masks are made by freeze-drying the mask ingredients into a fine powder, which can be mixed with water or other liquid before application. Lyophilized stock solution masks are formulated as a liquid solution that is freeze-dried into a solid form, which can be reconstituted with a liquid before use. Both types of freeze-drying masks offer convenience, longer shelf life, and easy transportation.</p></p>
