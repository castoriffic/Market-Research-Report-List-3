<p><p>Reactive thinners are categorized into two main types: monofunctional and multifunctional. Monofunctional reactive thinners are designed to react with a single component in a coating system, providing specific properties such as improved adhesion or flexibility. On the other hand, multifunctional reactive thinners are capable of reacting with multiple components, offering a wider range of benefits such as enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types cater to the diverse needs of the coatings industry, providing tailored solutions for various applications.</p></p>
