<p><p>Soluble fertilizers are categorized into different types based on their composition and function. NPK Water-Soluble fertilizers contain essential nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium in a soluble form for easy absorption by plants. Humic Acid Water-Soluble fertilizers are enriched with humic acid, which enhances soil structure and nutrient uptake. Amino Acid Water-Soluble fertilizers provide plants with organic nitrogen for improved growth and stress tolerance. Other types of soluble fertilizers may include micronutrient-rich formulations or specialty blends for specific plant needs.</p></p>
