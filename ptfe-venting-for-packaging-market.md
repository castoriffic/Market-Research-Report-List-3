<p><p>PTFE venting for packaging includes adhesive vents and vents without backing material. Adhesive vents use a pressure-sensitive adhesive for easy application on a variety of surfaces, allowing for the release of gases while maintaining a barrier against liquids. Vents without backing material are inserted directly into packaging materials, providing a discreet and effective way to release pressure and prevent package distortion. These venting solutions are essential for maintaining product integrity and extending shelf life in various industries.</p></p>
