<p><p>Silicon Germanium materials market can be categorized into three main types - Source Materials, Substrate Materials, and Epitaxial Wafers. Source materials refer to the raw materials used in the production of Silicon Germanium compounds. Substrate materials are the base materials on which the Silicon Germanium layers are deposited. Epitaxial wafers are the final products with the Silicon Germanium layers grown on top of a substrate.
