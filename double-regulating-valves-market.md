<p><p>Double regulating valves are used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems to regulate the flow of water in the system. These valves have two functions - balancing and throttling - hence the name double regulating valves. They are typically used in systems where it is important to maintain a consistent flow rate throughout the system.</p><p>The Double Regulating Valves Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.4% during the forecast period. The market growth is being driven by the increasing demand for energy-efficient HVAC systems in commercial and residential buildings. As energy costs continue to rise, building owners and operators are looking for ways to improve the efficiency of their HVAC systems. Double regulating valves help to optimize the flow of water in these systems, leading to energy savings and lower operating costs.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the double regulating valves market is the integration of smart technology. Manufacturers are incorporating sensors and actuators into their valves to provide real-time data on flow rates and system performance. This allows building operators to monitor and adjust the valves remotely, optimizing system efficiency and reducing maintenance costs. As the trend towards smart buildings continues to grow, the demand for smart double regulating valves is expected to increase as well.</p></p>
