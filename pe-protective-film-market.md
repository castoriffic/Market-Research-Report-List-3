<p><p>PE protective film is a type of film used to protect surfaces from scratches, dirt, and damage during transportation, storage, or installation. There are three main types based on the density of the polyethylene used: high-density PE protective film, medium-density PE protective film, and low-density PE protective film. High-density films offer high strength and durability, medium-density films offer a balance of strength and flexibility, and low-density films are more flexible and conformable to uneven surfaces. Each type is suitable for different applications based on the level of protection needed.</p></p>
