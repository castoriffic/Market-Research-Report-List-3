<p><p>Swing door sensors market offers various types based on product type such as surface contact sensors, recessed contact sensors, vanishing sensors, and vented sensors. These sensors utilize different technologies including active infrared, auto focus position sensitive detection (PSD), microwave, and others. Surface contact sensors are mounted on the surface of the door, while recessed sensors are installed inside the door frame. Vanishing sensors are discreet and blend with the door, and vented sensors allow for airflow while still detecting motion.</p></p>
