<p><p>The semiconductor exhaust gas treatment market utilizes various methods to remove harmful gases emitted during manufacturing processes. Direct incineration involves burning the gas, while activated carbon adsorption uses adsorbent materials to capture the gases. Biological oxidation utilizes microorganisms to degrade the gases. Additionally, there are other methods like absorption, chemical scrubbing, and condensation that help remove these gases. These techniques play a crucial role in maintaining air quality and reducing pollution in semiconductor manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
