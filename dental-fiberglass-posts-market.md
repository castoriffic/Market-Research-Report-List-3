<p><p>Dental fiberglass posts come in three main types: tapered, parallel, and other. Tapered posts gradually decrease in diameter from the top to the tip, providing better adaptation to the natural root canal shape. Parallel posts have a consistent diameter along the entire length, offering easier placement and removal. Other types may include variations like serrated or serrated-screw posts, which have unique designs for enhanced stability and retention. Each type caters to different clinical scenarios and preferences in dental restoration procedures.</p></p>
