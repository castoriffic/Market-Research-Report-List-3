<p><p>Distribution arresters are classified into three types based on their duty level. Light duty arresters are designed for residential and low-risk areas to provide basic protection against moderate surges. Normal duty arresters are suitable for commercial and industrial applications with higher risk levels, offering better surge protection capabilities. Heavy duty arresters are used in critical infrastructure and high-risk environments where maximum surge protection is necessary. Each type serves different purposes and provides varying levels of protection against overvoltage events in electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
