<p><p>AF Driver IC market can be segmented into three types: Open Loop Driver IC, Close Loop Driver IC, and OIS Driver IC. Close Loop Driver ICs offer better accuracy and precision by continuously adjusting the output based on feedback. OIS Driver ICs are specifically designed for optical image stabilization in cameras, providing smooth and stable images. Each type caters to different applications and requirements within the AF Driver IC market.</p></p>
