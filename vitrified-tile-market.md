<p><p>Vitrified tiles are ceramic tiles that have been through a process called vitrification, which involves the application of high heat and pressure. This process results in tiles that are highly durable, scratch-resistant, and have a low water absorption rate. Vitrified tiles are widely used in residential and commercial spaces due to their long-lasting nature and easy maintenance.</p><p>The global vitrified tile market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. The growth of the market can be attributed to the increasing demand for aesthetically pleasing and durable flooring options in residential and commercial spaces. The rise in construction activities, especially in developing countries, is also driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the vitrified tile market is the increasing popularity of large format tiles. These tiles are preferred for their seamless look and easy maintenance. Additionally, digital printing technology is being increasingly used in the production of vitrified tiles, allowing for a wide range of design options for consumers. With the growing focus on sustainability, eco-friendly vitrified tiles made from recycled materials are also becoming popular in the market.</p></p>
