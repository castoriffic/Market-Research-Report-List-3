<p><p>The Vacuum Turbo Pumps Market is segmented into three types - Magnetically Suspended Type, Oil Lubricated Type, and Others. Magnetically Suspended Type pumps use magnetic bearings for levitation and rotation, reducing friction and wear. Oil Lubricated Type pumps use oil for lubrication and cooling, suitable for high vacuum applications. The Others category includes different types of turbo pumps that do not fall into the above two categories. Each type offers unique advantages and is used in various industries for creating and maintaining vacuum levels.</p></p>
