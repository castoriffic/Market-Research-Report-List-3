<p><p>High Voltage Motor Pt100 temperature sensors can be categorized into two main types: Bearing Type and Stator Embedded Type. </p><p>Bearing Type sensors are installed directly into the bearings of a motor, allowing for accurate temperature monitoring at the critical components. </p><p>Stator Embedded Type sensors are integrated into the stator windings of the motor, providing real-time temperature data for efficient operation and preventing overheating. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safe and reliable performance of high voltage motors.</p></p>
