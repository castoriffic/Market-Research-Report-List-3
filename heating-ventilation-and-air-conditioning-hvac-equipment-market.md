<p><p>Split HVAC systems are the most common type, consisting of an outdoor compressor unit and an indoor air handler. Hybrid split systems combine a gas furnace with an electric heat pump for improved efficiency. Duct-free systems use individual units for each room, ideal for spaces lacking ductwork. Packaged heating and air systems contain all components in one unit, suitable for commercial or larger residential buildings. Each type of HVAC system offers different benefits based on the specific needs of the building.</p></p>
