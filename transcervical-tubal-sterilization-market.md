<p><p>Transcervical Tubal Sterilization market includes two primary types: Tubal Ligation and Transcervical Implant. Tubal ligation is a surgical procedure where the fallopian tubes are cut, tied, or blocked for permanent sterilization. On the other hand, transcervical implants involve inserting a small device through the cervix to block the fallopian tubes, preventing fertilization. These methods provide women with a long-term contraceptive option that is less invasive than traditional sterilization surgeries.</p></p>
