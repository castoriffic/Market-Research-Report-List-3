<p><strong>The Arrestor Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Line</li><li>Substation</li><li>Distribution Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Arrestors are commonly used in three main applications within the electrical power industry: transmission lines, substations, and distribution lines. In transmission lines, arrestors protect the line from lightning strikes and high voltage surges. In substations, they safeguard important equipment such as transformers and circuit breakers. Similarly, in distribution lines, arrestors protect against transient overvoltages. Overall, arrestors play a vital role in ensuring the reliability and safety of electrical power systems across all three applications.</p></p>
