<p><p>High Melt Strength Polypropylene (HMSPP) is a type of polypropylene with enhanced strength and impact resistance, making it ideal for a variety of applications. It is available in two main types: homopolymer and copolymer. Homopolymer HMSPP is made from a single type of monomer and offers high stiffness and heat resistance. Copolymer HMSPP, on the other hand, incorporates multiple types of monomers, providing improved flexibility and impact strength. Both types of HMSPP cater to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
