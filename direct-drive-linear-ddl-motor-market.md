<p><ul><li>Ironcore Linear Motors</li><li>Ironless Linear Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Drive Linear (DDL) Motors are used in various applications and are broadly classified into two types: Ironcore Linear Motors and Ironless Linear Motors. Ironcore Linear Motors feature a coil with iron laminations, offering high force output and are suitable for high force applications. On the other hand, Ironless Linear Motors are known for their smooth and precise movement without cogging, making them suitable for applications where smooth motion is critical, such as in semiconductor manufacturing and medical devices.</p></p>
