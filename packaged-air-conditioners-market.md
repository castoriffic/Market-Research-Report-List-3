<p><p>Packaged Air Conditioners are available in two main types based on the type of condenser they use: Packaged AC with Water Cooled Condenser and Packaged AC with Air Cooled Condenser. Packaged AC with Water Cooled Condenser systems use water to remove heat from the refrigerant, making them more efficient in hot climates. On the other hand, Packaged AC with Air Cooled Condenser systems use air to cool the refrigerant, making them more suitable for moderate climates and locations where water availability may be limited.</p></p>
