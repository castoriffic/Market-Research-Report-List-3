<p><p>IoT management software encompasses two main types: cloud-based and web-based markets. Cloud-based IoT management software refers to solutions that are hosted and accessed through the internet, eliminating the need for on-premise infrastructure. It offers flexibility and scalability, allowing users to manage their IoT devices from anywhere. On the other hand, web-based IoT management software refers to solutions that are accessed through web browsers. While not as flexible or scalable as cloud-based options, web-based software still provides users with remote management capabilities within their network. Both types offer distinct advantages based on specific business requirements.</p></p>
