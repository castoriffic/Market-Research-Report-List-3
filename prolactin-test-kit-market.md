<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prolactin test kits are commonly available in two types: time-resolved immunofluorescence and chemiluminescence immunoassay. Time-resolved immunofluorescence kits utilize fluorescent labels to detect prolactin levels in patient samples, providing accurate and quick results. On the other hand, chemiluminescence immunoassay kits use chemical reactions to measure prolactin levels, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Both types of kits are widely used in clinical settings for diagnosing conditions related to prolactin levels such as infertility and hormonal imbalances.</p></p>
