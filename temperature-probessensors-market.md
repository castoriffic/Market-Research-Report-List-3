<p><p>Temperature probes/sensors market is segmented into two types: non-contact temperature sensors and contact temperature sensors. Non-contact temperature sensors detect temperature without physical contact with the object using infrared technology, suitable for moving objects or hazardous environments. Contact temperature sensors require direct contact with the object to measure temperature accurately, commonly used in industrial applications. Both types cater to specific needs and industries, offering solutions for precise temperature monitoring and control in various settings.</p></p>
