<p><p>Car video monitors are available in two main market types - OEM and aftermarket. OEM refers to monitors that are installed by the vehicle manufacturer as part of the original equipment. These monitors are designed to seamlessly integrate with the car's interior and are often more expensive. Aftermarket monitors, on the other hand, are purchased separately and installed by third-party manufacturers or retailers. They offer a wider range of options and prices, but may not always match the aesthetics of the car as well as OEM monitors.</p></p>
