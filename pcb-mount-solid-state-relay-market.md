<p><p>PCB Mount Solid State Relays (SSRs) are categorized into three main types based on their output: AC Output SSRs, DC Output SSRs, and AC/DC Output SSRs. AC Output SSRs are used to switch AC loads, while DC Output SSRs are designed for DC loads. AC/DC Output SSRs are versatile relays capable of switching both AC and DC loads. Each type serves specific applications and industries, such as industrial automation, heating systems, and motor control, providing efficient and reliable switching solutions for various electronic devices.</p></p>
