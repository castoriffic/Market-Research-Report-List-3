<p><ul><li>Three Phase</li><li>Two Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filter reactors are commonly classified based on the number of phases they operate in. Three-phase filter reactors are designed for three-phase power systems, providing filtering and protection for systems with three power conductors. Two-phase filter reactors are used in two-phase power systems, offering similar filtering and protection capabilities for systems with two power conductors. Both types of filter reactors play a crucial role in providing efficient power quality and protecting electrical equipment from disturbances and harmonics in their respective power systems.</p></p>
