<p><p>Medium power relays are available in two main types in the market - Closed Type and Open Type. Closed type relays are enclosed in a protective casing, providing better protection against dust, moisture, and other environmental factors. Open type relays, on the other hand, do not have a protective casing and are more suitable for applications where heat dissipation is a priority. Both types cater to different needs and requirements, offering users a variety of options to choose from based on their specific usage scenarios.</p></p>
