<p><p>A plate-fin heat exchanger is a type of heat exchanger that utilizes a series of finned chambers to transfer heat between two fluids. The fluids flow on opposite sides of the finned chambers, with one fluid being heated while the other is cooled. The fins increase the surface area available for heat transfer, maximizing the efficiency of the exchange process.</p><p>The plate-fin heat exchanger market is experiencing significant growth worldwide.
