<p><p>Color coated sheet for household appliances comes in two main types: PCM (Pre-coated Metal) and VCM (PVC Coated Metal). PCM sheets are coated with specially designed paint that provides durability and scratch resistance, making them ideal for use in appliances like refrigerators and washing machines. VCM sheets, on the other hand, are coated with PVC film, which offers a glossy finish and high chemical resistance, making them suitable for appliances like microwave ovens and air conditioners. Both types of color coated sheets cater to different needs in the household appliance market.</p></p>
