<p><p>Common sucker rod market types include C Grade, D Grade, K Grade, and KD Grade. C Grade is the most commonly used rod with moderate strength and flexibility. D Grade rods have higher tensile strength and are often used in deeper wells. K Grade rods are high-strength and are used in harsh conditions or high-pressure wells. KD Grade rods combine the advantages of both K Grade and D Grade, making them versatile and suitable for a wide range of well conditions.</p></p>
