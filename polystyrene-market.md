<p><p>Polystyrene is a versatile thermoplastic polymer used in various industries, with two main market types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a transparent, rigid plastic commonly used in packaging, household items, and electronics. HIPS, on the other hand, is a tougher material that is impact-resistant and widely used in food packaging, construction, and consumer goods. Both types of polystyrene offer different properties and applications, catering to a wide range of industries.</p></p>
