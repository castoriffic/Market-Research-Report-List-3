<p><p>Aromatic extract market types refer to the two main processes used in the production of aromatic extracts: Direct Chlorination (DC) process and Hydrochlorination (HC) process. In the DC process, chlorine is directly introduced to the aromatic compound to create the extract, while in the HC process, hydrochloric acid is used instead. Both processes yield different characteristics and qualities in the aromatic extracts, leading to varied market demands based on the specific applications and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
