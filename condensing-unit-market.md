<p><ul><li>Air-cooled Condensing Unit</li><li>Water-cooled Condensing Unit</li><li>Evaporative Condensing Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Condensing units are essential components in refrigeration systems that help remove heat from the system. The air-cooled condensing unit uses air as a cooling medium, making it suitable for indoor installations. Water-cooled condensing units use water as a cooling medium, making them ideal for areas where air cooling is challenging. Evaporative condensing units use water and air to cool the refrigerant, offering energy efficiency and lower operating costs. Each type of condensing unit offers unique advantages depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
