<p><p>Rear dash cameras come in two main types: single channel dash cameras and multi-channel dash cameras. Single channel dash cameras are designed to capture footage from only one perspective, typically the rear of the vehicle. On the other hand, multi-channel dash cameras can capture footage from multiple angles simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive view of the surroundings. Both types of cameras are essential for enhancing safety and security on the road by providing valuable footage in case of accidents or incidents.</p></p>
