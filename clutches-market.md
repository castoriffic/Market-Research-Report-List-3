<p><p>The Clutches market consists of three main types: Manual, Semi-automatic, and Full-automatic. Manual clutches require the driver to fully engage and disengage the clutch pedal to shift gears. Semi-automatic clutches combine manual and automatic functions, allowing the driver to shift gears without fully engaging the clutch. Full-automatic clutches do not require any input from the driver to shift gears, making driving easier and more convenient. Each type of clutch caters to different preferences and driving styles, offering a range of options for consumers in the market.</p></p>
