<p><ul><li>Fiber Duodenal Endoscope</li><li>Electronic Duodenal Endoscope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Duodenal Endoscope market consists of two types: Fiber Duodenal Endoscope and Electronic Duodenal Endoscope. </p><p>Fiber Duodenal Endoscopes are equipped with optical fibers that transmit light to illuminate the duodenum and relay images to the eyepiece or camera. These endoscopes are more affordable and flexible, but they may require additional light sources.</p><p>Electronic Duodenal Endoscopes, on the other hand, use electronic image sensors and technology to capture and transmit real-time images of the duodenum. These endoscopes offer high-resolution images and are more durable, but they can be more expensive.</p><p>Both types aim to assist in diagnosing and treating duodenal conditions by allowing healthcare professionals to visualize the duodenum internally.</p></p>
