<p><strong>The Petroleum Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C5 resins</li><li>C9 resins</li><li>C5/C9 resins</li><li>Hydrogenated resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Petroleum resins are classified into four main types based on their chemical composition and properties. C5 resins are derived from C5 fractions of crude oil and are known for their excellent tackiness and adhesion properties. C9 resins are produced from C9 fractions and are valued for their high heat resistance and color stability. C5/C9 resins are a blend of C5 and C9 resins, offering a balance of properties. Hydrogenated resins are modified to improve thermal stability and light color, making them suitable for various applications.</p></p>
