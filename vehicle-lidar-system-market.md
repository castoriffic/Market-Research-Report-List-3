<p><p>Vehicle Lidar Systems are crucial for autonomous vehicles, drone navigation, and 3D mapping. There are two main types of Lidar systems: Mechanical Lidar and Solid State Lidar. Mechanical Lidar systems use spinning mirrors to create a 360-degree field of view, while Solid State Lidar systems use fixed components, such as microelectromechanical or optical phased arrays, to steer laser beams. Solid State Lidar systems are becoming increasingly popular due to their compact size, lower cost, and improved reliability compared to Mechanical Lidar systems.</p></p>
