<p><p>White Portland Cement comes in three main market types: Normal, High Early Strength, and Sulfate Resistant. Normal white Portland cement is suitable for general construction purposes. High Early Strength white Portland cement is designed for projects requiring faster setting times and high early strength development. Sulfate Resistant white Portland cement is used in environments with high sulfate content in the soil or water, such as marine structures and sewage systems. Each market type offers specific properties to meet the varying needs of construction projects.</p></p>
