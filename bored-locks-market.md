<p><p>Bored locks are categorized into two main market types - cylindrical and tubular. Cylindrical bored locks are the most common type, with a circular shape and a keyhole located on the outside knob. They are typically used in residential and commercial settings. Tubular bored locks have a similar design but with a more compact cylindrical shape, making them suitable for applications where space is limited, such as in office furniture or cabinets. Both types offer security and convenience for various locking needs.</p></p>
