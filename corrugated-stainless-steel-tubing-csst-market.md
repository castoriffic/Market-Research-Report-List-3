<p><p>Corrugated Stainless Steel Tubing (CSST) is available in two main market types: 304 SS and 316 SS. 304 SS CSST is made of a common stainless steel grade that offers good corrosion resistance and affordability, making it suitable for general applications. On the other hand, 316 SS CSST is made of a higher grade stainless steel that provides superior corrosion resistance and strength, making it ideal for more demanding environments and applications where high performance is required.</p></p>
